About

王子瑜，居住在中国的独立动画创作者，2003年出生于中国长春，2025年毕业于中国传媒大学动画专业。她基于现实的过往经历，以女性视角和超现实的风格出发进行实验叙事的逐帧动画创作。她的作品曾入围国内外多个影节展出，包括萨格勒布动画节、荷兰 Kaboom 动画节、加拿大幻想电影节（国际B类）、Feinaki Animation Festival、俄罗斯失眠动画节等。她的创作聚焦情感、亲密关系、私人话题，通过动画的创作形式连接现实与想象。

Ziyu Wang is an independent animation artist based in China. Born in 2003 in Changchun, she graduated in 2025 from the Animation Department of the Communication University of China. Her work draws on lived experiences and explores themes of emotions, intimacy, and personal narratives through experimental, surreal, frame-by-frame animation. Her films have been selected for Animafest Zagreb, Kaboom Animation Festival, Fantasia International Film Festival (FIAPF B-category), Feinaki Animation Festival, and Insomnia Animation Festival.
